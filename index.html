Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Noise Mate

Can I share reports with my landlord or council?

Yes. Noise Mate is designed to help you document noise accurately so you can share clear evidence with landlords, housing associations, or local councils to help resolve persistent noise issues.

What's the accuracy of the decibel measurements?

Noise Mate provides reliable decibel readings using your device's built-in microphone, suitable for showing noise trends and levels over time. It is a powerful tool for documenting disturbances in a consistent way.

Can I use this on multiple devices?

Absolutely. Your account syncs across all devices (iOS, Android, Web). All your recordings and reports are accessible from anywhere with secure end-to-end encryption.

What happens if I'm offline?

Recordings work completely offline. Everything is saved locally on your device and automatically synced to our secure servers when you reconnect. No risk of losing evidence.

How long is my data retained?

You control your data retention. Records can be stored indefinitely on our secure servers. We support deletion requests anytime, fully compliant with GDPR.

How do I send my data to the council?

You can easily export your noise logs as a professional PDF report and email it directly to your local Environmental Health department or housing officer. The reports include timelines and noise levels to help explain the situation clearly.

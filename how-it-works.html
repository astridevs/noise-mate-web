Step 1: Forensic Monitoring

Zero-Latency Calibration

When you launch the Noise Mate meter, our engine begins sampling external audio at 44.1kHz. We don't just measure volume; we track Peak Decibels (dB), Average Levels, and Frequency Oscillations. This allows the app to distinguish between a passing car and a persistent nuisance noise.

Peak Detection

Our algorithm catches instantaneous noise spikes that standard recorders miss, ensuring 'thumping' or 'impact' noises are recorded at their true intensity.

Smart Thresholds

Set custom sensitivity levels. Noise Mate can run in the background and only trigger recording when the noise exceeds your predefined 'Nuisance Threshold'.

Step 2: Authenticated Documentation

Binding Truth to Metadata

A recording alone is weak evidence. Noise Mate builds a "Case File" by binding your recordings to high-integrity metadata that landlords and councils trust. Every log captures the exact second of the event, duration, and environmental tags.

GPS Benchmarking

Every recording is stamped with verified GPS coordinates, proving the incident occurred at your registered address or a specific location of interest.

Taxonomy Tagging

Categorize events instantly: construction, pets, parties, or DIY. This tagging allows for "Pattern Recognition" reports that show authorities when and how often disturbances occur.

Local-First Forensic Vault

We believe your data is yours alone. Noise Mate uses a Local-First architecture. All decibel processing and audio capture happen on your device's raw hardware. No audio is transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable Cloud Sync for your Case Reports.

Step 3: The Share Link Portal

Professional Evidence Distribution

Once your evidence is collected, Noise Mate simplifies discovery. Instead of sending bulky email attachments, subscribers can generate a Secure Share Link. This allows third parties to interact with your data in a controlled environment.

2-Factor Access (Ultimate)

Recipients must enter a unique Case ID and a 6-digit PIN. You maintain full control: revoke access or set expiry dates on any evidence link at any time.

Interactive Web Viewer

Landlords and Officers can play audio, view intensity charts, and audit your photo evidence directly in their browser without installing the app.

Expert Capture Tips

Clear Path

Ensure your phone microphone is not obstructed by a case or your hand. Position the device 1-2 meters away from the source wall for best surface-noise capture.

Reference Point

Capture a 30-second 'Ambient Log' when it's quiet. This provides a baseline for council officers to see just how much louder the nuisance noise is compared to your normal environment.

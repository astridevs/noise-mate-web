Simply Noise Mate

Turn sound into proof in 4 easy steps. No complex settings, just clear evidence.

Step 01

Open the App

Launch the Noise Mate meter to see live decibel levels in your room. It works instantly without any setup required.

Step 02

Record the Noise

Tap record when the disturbance starts. Noise Mate captures high-quality audio so you have proof of the exact noise you're hearing.

Step 03

Add Context

Quickly tag what caused the noise (like music or building work) and add any notes. We automatically save the time and location for you.

Step 04

Send Your Report

Generate a professional report with one tap. Send it to your landlord or local council as a PDF or a secure private link.

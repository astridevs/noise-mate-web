How Noise Mate Works

Turn your smartphone into a professional noise monitoring and evidence collection tool.

The 3-Step Evidence Guide

1

Monitor & Record

Launch the Noise Mate meter to track decibel levels in real-time. When a disturbance occurs, the app automatically captures high-fidelity audio spikes, ensuring you have the auditory proof needed for your case.

2

Document & Tag

Organise your recordings with vital context. Tag each incident with its source (e.g., loud music, barking dog), severity level, and specific location markers. Our system automatically includes time-stamps and duration logs.

3

Export & Share

Generate professional, evidence-grade PDF reports with a single tap. These reports are formatted to meet the standards of UK landlords, local councils, and legal proceedings, making it easier to present your evidence clearly.

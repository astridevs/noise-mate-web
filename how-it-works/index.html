How Noise Mate Works

Turn your smartphone into a professional noise monitoring and evidence collection tool with our legally robust documentation engine.

1

Monitor & Record

Launch the Noise Mate meter to track decibel levels in real-time. Our advanced monitoring engine detects frequency spikes and temporal patterns. When a disturbance occurs, the app automatically captures high-fidelity audio evidence, ensuring you have the auditory proof needed for your case. All recordings are encrypted and timestamped at the point of origin.

2

Document & Tag

Organise your recordings with vital context that authorities require. Tag each incident with its specific source (e.g., loud music, construction activity, barking dog) and assign severity levels. Our system automatically attaches verified metadata including location markers, atmospheric conditions, and duration logs to build a forensic timeline.

3

Export & Share

Generate professional, evidence-grade PDF reports with a single tap. These reports are meticulously formatted to meet the strict standards of UK landlords, local councils, environmental health officers, and legal proceedings. Upgrade to Ultimate to generate secure access PINs for recipients to view your evidence portal directly.

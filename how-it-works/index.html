Real-time Decibel Engine

Acoustic Visualisation

Impact Indicators

Categorise noise from 'Quiet' to 'Dangerous' automatically based on standard acoustic disturbance guidelines.

Forensic Capture

Auditory Fingerprinting

Capture high-fidelity m4a recordings of the disturbance. Audio is securely stored and can be looped or scrubbed for detailed review.

Visual Documentation

Verified Metadata

Strategic Case Management

The Complaint Wizard

Structure your logs into cohesive 'Cases'. Our guided wizard helps you identify the noise type, the specific source, and the intended recipient (Council, Landlord, etc.).

Temporal Pattern Mapping

Identify 'Worst Hours'—whether the noise is night-time, afternoon, or persistent all day. This pattern data is critical for council investigations.

Frequency Tracking

Document if the noise is Daily, Weekly, or Irregular. We build a frequency heat-map that authorities use to determine the severity of the nuisance.

Recipient Directives

Manage unique cases for different recipients. Keep your landlord complaint separate from your environmental health logs automatically.

Authority Reporting

UK-Standard PDF Engine

Generate reports professionally formatted for UK Councils and Environmental Health Officers. No more handwritten diaries.

Automated Email Drafting
